<p><p>The in-ceiling speaker market consists of two primary types: active and passive speakers. Active in-ceiling speakers contain built-in amplifiers, allowing for easy installation and setup, as they require only a power source and audio input. Passive in-ceiling speakers, on the other hand, require an external amplifier to function, providing greater flexibility in audio configuration and potentially higher sound quality. Both types cater to different user needs, from simple home setups to more complex audio systems, enhancing audio experiences in various environments.</p></p>
