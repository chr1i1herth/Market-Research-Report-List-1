<p><p>The soap colorants market is primarily divided into oil-based and water-based colorants. Oil-based soap colorants are derived from oils and are ideal for use in cold process soaps, providing vibrant colors that disperse well in fatty bases. In contrast, water-based soap colorants are suitable for melt-and-pour or liquid soaps, offering ease of use and a wide range of hues without affecting the soap's consistency. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, enhancing the visual appeal of handmade soaps.</p></p>
