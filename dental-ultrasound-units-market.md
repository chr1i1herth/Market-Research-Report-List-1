<p><strong>The Dental Ultrasound Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental ultrasound units market is primarily categorized into two types: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric devices. Magnetostrictive units utilize a metal stack to create ultrasonic vibrations, effectively removing debris and calculus during dental procedures. They are known for their versatility and efficiency. In contrast, piezoelectric units use ceramic crystals to generate ultrasonic waves, allowing for precise and controlled scaling. Both types are favored for their ability to enhance periodontal treatments and improve patient comfort while ensuring effective dental care.</p></p>
