<p><p>The fusion splicer market is primarily divided into two types: single fiber fusion splicers and mass fusion splicers. Single fiber fusion splicers are designed for precise splicing of individual optical fibers, suitable for applications requiring high accuracy and reliability. In contrast, mass fusion splicers can splice multiple fibers simultaneously, making them ideal for large-scale projects and installations, such as telecommunications and data networks. Both types cater to various needs in the fiber optics industry, enhancing connectivity and signal quality.</p></p>
