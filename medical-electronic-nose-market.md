<p><p>The Medical Electronic Nose market comprises various sensor technologies, including Metal Oxide Semiconductor (MOS), Conducting Polymer (CP), Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM), and Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW). MOS sensors detect volatile organic compounds through changes in resistance. CP sensors utilize conductivity alterations in response to gases. QCM measures mass changes on a crystal surface, allowing for precise detection. SAW sensors use acoustic wave propagation to identify specific gas concentrations, enhancing diagnostic applications. Each type offers unique advantages for medical diagnostics and disease detection.</p></p>
