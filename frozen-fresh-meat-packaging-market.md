<p><ul><li>Vacuum Packaging</li><li>Modified Atmosphere Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Frozen Fresh Meat Packaging market primarily utilizes two types of packaging methods: vacuum packaging and modified atmosphere packaging (MAP). Vacuum packaging involves sealing meat in an airtight bag, removing oxygen to extend shelf life and preserve freshness. On the other hand, MAP replaces the air inside the packaging with a specific mixture of gases, altering the atmospheric composition to slow spoilage and maintain quality. Both methods enhance the durability and safety of frozen meat during storage and transportation.</p></p>
