<p><p>The vaso transplantation market consists of three main types: EPTFE vaso transplantation, polyester vaso transplantation, and PTFE vaso transplantation. EPTFE (expanded polytetrafluoroethylene) offers enhanced biocompatibility and flexibility, making it suitable for various vascular surgeries. Polyester vaso transplantation, known for its durability and strength, is often used in reconstructive procedures. PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) provides excellent chemical resistance and is commonly employed in vascular grafts. Each type serves specific clinical needs in vascular reconstruction and restoration, catering to diverse patient requirements.</p></p>
