<p><p>Brain PET MRI imaging systems are essential for advanced neuroimaging, combining positron emission tomography (PET) and magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) for enhanced brain activity analysis. Traditional PET-MRI systems use helium-cooled magnets, ensuring high-resolution imaging but often face supply and cost challenges. In contrast, helium-free PET-MRI systems utilize alternative cooling technologies, making them more sustainable and cost-effective while maintaining performance. Both types cater to the growing demand for sophisticated diagnostic tools in neurology and research, each offering distinct advantages.</p></p>
