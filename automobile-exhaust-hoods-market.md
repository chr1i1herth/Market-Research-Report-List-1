<p><p>The automobile exhaust hoods market features two primary types: single shell and double shell hoods. Single shell hoods consist of a single layer that captures exhaust emissions directly from the vehicle, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, double shell hoods feature an additional outer layer, enhancing insulation and thermal protection while improving durability. This design reduces heat dissipation and noise, making double shell hoods suitable for high-performance or heavy-duty applications, catering to a broader range of industry needs.</p></p>
