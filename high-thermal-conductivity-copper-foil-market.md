<p><p>The high thermal conductivity copper foil market comprises two primary types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced through mechanical processes, offering excellent thermal and electrical conductivity, suitable for various industrial applications. Conversely, electrolytic copper foil is made using electrochemical methods, resulting in finer grain structures and superior performance in flexible electronics and battery applications. Both types play crucial roles in sectors like electronics, renewable energy, and automotive, catering to the demand for efficient thermal management solutions.</p></p>
