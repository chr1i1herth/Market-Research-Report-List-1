<p><ul><li>BLE Keys</li><li>NFC Keys</li><li>UWB Keys</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital car keys market primarily features three technology types: BLE (Bluetooth Low Energy) keys, NFC (Near Field Communication) keys, and UWB (Ultra-Wideband) keys. BLE keys enable long-range communication for remote access and convenience, ideal for smartphones to unlock cars without physical keys. NFC keys offer proximity access, requiring close contact for unlocking, enhancing security. UWB keys provide precise location tracking and secure communication, allowing for features like keyless entry with improved accuracy, making it a preferred choice for high-end vehicles.</p></p>
