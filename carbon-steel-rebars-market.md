<p><p>The carbon steel rebars market primarily consists of two main types: deformed steel and mild steel. Deformed steel rebars feature surface ridges, enhancing their grip within concrete, making them ideal for high-strength applications like bridges and buildings. Mild steel rebars, characterized by their smooth surface, are more ductile and suitable for low-stress applications, such as residential structures. Both types play crucial roles in construction, providing essential tensile strength and stability to reinforced concrete structures.</p></p>
