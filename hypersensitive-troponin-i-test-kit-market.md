<p><p>The Hypersensitive Troponin I Test Kit Market consists of two primary types: Time-resolved Immunofluorescence and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay. Time-resolved Immunofluorescence utilizes fluorescent labels and time-resolved detection to measure troponin levels with high sensitivity. In contrast, Chemiluminescence Immunoassay employs luminescent reactions to detect troponin, allowing for rapid and accurate results. Both methods are critical for identifying myocardial injury, facilitating early diagnosis of conditions such as acute coronary syndrome, and enhancing patient management in cardiac care.</p></p>
