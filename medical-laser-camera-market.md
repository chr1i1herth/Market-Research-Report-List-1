<p><p>The medical laser camera market is segmented into dry type and wet type categories based on the medium used for imaging. Dry type cameras use solid-state sensors and capture images without the need for liquid chemicals, making them more efficient and less messy. Wet type cameras, on the other hand, require photographic film or a liquid medium for image development, often providing higher resolution but involving more chemical processes. Each type caters to different clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
