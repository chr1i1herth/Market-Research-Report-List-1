<p><p>The 3D mammography machines market encompasses several types, each utilizing different technologies. Computed radiography machines rely on imaging plates to capture mammograms, offering enhanced image storage and retrieval. Flat panel machines use digital detectors to provide high-resolution images with lower radiation doses. CMOS flat machines leverage complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, delivering efficient imaging with reduced power consumption and greater sensitivity. These varied technologies cater to the evolving demands for accurate breast cancer screening and diagnosis, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
