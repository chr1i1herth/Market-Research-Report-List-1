<p><ul><li>Moving Grate</li><li>Rotary-kiln</li><li>Fluidized Bed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Municipal Solid Waste Incineration market is categorized into three main types: Moving Grate, Rotary Kiln, and Fluidized Bed systems. Moving Grate incinerators utilize a conveyor mechanism to move waste through the combustion chamber, promoting efficient burning. Rotary Kilns rotate to provide uniform heat and prolonged combustion time, effectively handling heterogeneous waste. Fluidized Bed systems utilize air bubbles to suspend and fluidize waste, ensuring uniform temperature distribution and efficient combustion, making each type suited for different waste compositions and processing needs.</p></p>
